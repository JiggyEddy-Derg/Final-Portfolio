Who am I?

My name is Edward, though i prefer to be refered to as Eddie. I am Graphic Designer and Illustrator born and raised in Coney Island. I enjoy doing many things, which include:

  1. Drawing
  2. Dancing
  3. Playing arcade games
  4. Designing brands

I have been in the creative space for many years. My creative awakening started with books about dragons and pokemon. Growing up, these two things really brought my creative mind to life. Being able to think of new Pokemon or dragon designs I would love to see in person.
